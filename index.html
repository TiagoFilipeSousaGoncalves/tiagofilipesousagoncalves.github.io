Tiago Gonçalves

Porto, Portugal tiago.f.goncalves@inesctec.pt

PhD Student at FEUP | Research Assistant at INESC TEC | Machine Learning and Deep Learning Enthusiast



I received my MSc in Bioengineering (Biomedical Engineering) from Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto (FEUP) in 2019. Currently, I am a PhD Candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at FEUP and a research assistant at the Centre for Telecommunications and Multimedia of INESC TEC with the Visual Computing & Machine Intelligence (VCMI) Research Group.
My research interests include machine learning, explainable artificial intelligence (in-model approaches), computer vision, medical decision support systems, and machine learning deployment.

Besides working, I enjoy practising Karate-Do Shotokai or reading about life, philosophy, economy, politics or fundamental science.
Moreover, I do not refuse a good TV series or a good film (preferably at the cinema).
