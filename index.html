Tiago Gonçalves

Porto, Portugal tiago.f.goncalves@inesctec.pt

PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering | Trustworthy Artificial Intelligence Enthusiast



I received my MSc in Bioengineering (Biomedical Engineering) from Faculdade de Engenharia da Universidade do Porto (FEUP) in 2019 and my PhD in Electrical and Computer Engineering in 2025 from the same institution. I am a research assistant at the Centre for Telecommunications and Multimedia of INESC TEC with the Visual Computing & Machine Intelligence (VCMI) Research Group. My research interests include machine learning, explainable artificial intelligence (in-model approaches), computer vision, medical decision support systems, and machine learning deployment.

Besides working, I enjoy practising Karate-Do Shotokai or reading about life, philosophy, economy, politics or fundamental science.
Moreover, I do not refuse a good TV series or a good film (preferably at the cinema).
